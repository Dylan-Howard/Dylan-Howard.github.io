<h4>Entering A Place of Worship</h4>
<hr>
<p class="quote">GOD OF ALL GLORY,</p>
<p class="quote">We gather to worship You by meeting with You through song, hearing and learning from Your Word, and responding with our lives.</p>
<p class="quote">Father, by Your grace, You’ve given us Your Word. Every good and perfect gift is from above and among the chiefest is Your Word. Perfect, and infallible.</p>
<p class="quote">Word Made Flesh, be our focus, and our glory. Let us remember Your righteous life, loving sacrifice, and mighty resurrection.</p>
<p class="quote">Spirit of God, You breathed life into us in salvation, and this same breath lives in this Word. Empower me to teach and others to hear. Let us respond in obedience, trust, and delight.</p>
<p class="quote">I ask this for Your glory among all people, forever and ever, amen.</p>

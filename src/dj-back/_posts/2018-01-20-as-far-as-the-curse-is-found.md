<h4>As Far As The Curse Is Found</h4>
<hr>
<p class="quote">Holy God, Creator of all,</p>
<p class="quote">with us, He walked around.</p>
<p class="quote">Alas, no more, considering our fall,</p>
<p class="quote">as far as the curse is found.</p>

<p class="quote">Immersed forever in afflictions and woes.</p>
<p class="quote">Captives of evil, our hearts are bound.</p>
<p class="quote">Death and despair follow as shadows</p>
<p class="quote">as far as the curse is found.</p>

<p class="quote">Incarnate God, His life to give</p>
<p class="quote">with thorns, was crowned.</p>
<p class="quote">In death, He hung, that we may live</p>
<p class="quote">as far as the curse is found.</p>

<p class="quote">Onward to every nation spread from the tower</p>
<p class="quote">though trial and snares abound.</p>
<p class="quote">We’ll rest in Your Spirit’s holding power</p>
<p class="quote">as far as the curse is found.</p>

<p class="quote">On that final day</p>
<p class="quote">when trumpets sound,</p>
<p class="quote">with our Redeemer we’ll sing</p>
<p class="quote">as far as the curse was found.</p>

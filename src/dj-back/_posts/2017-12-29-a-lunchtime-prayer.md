<h4>At a Lunchtime Meal</h4>
<hr>
<p class="quote">GREAT PROVIDER,</p>
<p class="quote">You called me to rise once again this morning, to go out, to labor, to communicate, to learn, to study, to advance Your kingdom, to grow in holiness.</p>
<p class="quote">Now, I sit to enjoy this meal You’ve given me. By Your grace alone, I am able to sit here, to enjoy this food, to have my body restored with nourishment and grown.</p>
<p class="quote">This food doesn’t need flavors, nor does my tongue need taste. Yet You’ve richly blessed me with this gift. Let me enjoy this food. The flavors, the texture, to temperature. Let it remind me of You abundant goodness–the gifts that You’ve lavished me with, especially, the gift of Christ.</p>
<p class="quote">His blood is the blood of the incarnate God–infinite in value. How great the cost of my sin!</p>
<p class="quote">O God, let me walk in humility, covered in Christ’s righteousness, being conformed to His likeness, rejoicing in the goodness and glory of Your gospel.</p>

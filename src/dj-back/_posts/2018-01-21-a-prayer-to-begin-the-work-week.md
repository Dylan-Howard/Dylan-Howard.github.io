<h4>A Prayer To Begin The Work Week</h4>
<hr>
<p class="quote">Bondservants, obey your earthly masters with fear and trembling, with a sincere heart, as you would Christ, not by the way of eye-service, as people-pleasers, but as bondservants of Christ, doing the will of God from the heart, rendering service with a good will as to the Lord and not to man, knowing that whatever good anyone does, this he will receive back from the Lord, whether he is a bondservant or is free.</p>
<p class="quote">Ephesians 6:5-8</p>
<hr>
<p class="quote">CREATOR AND SUSTAINER OF ALL,</p>
<p class="quote">You’ve continued to uphold Your creation into this new week. By Your power, You’ve given me vitality this morning. You’ve bid me to rise, and I did.</p>
<p class="quote">I am dependent on You to sustain me this week, to respond to the truths You’ve shown me this Lord’s Day, to honor You in my actions, to love my family well, to glorify Christ in all my deeds¬–especially in the deeds of my vocation.</p>
<p class="quote">I praise You for the vocation with which You’ve provided me. By Your grace, You designed labor for our good and Your glory. Let my labor be saturated in Your pleasure. By Your power, help me to submit to my boss and, in so doing, display Christ’s submission to the Father.</p>
<p class="quote">Though I am weak in my flesh, let Your joy in my labor empower me to serve with a good attitude, as to You, not to man. Let this joy overflow in glad service, enabling me to make disciples through bold and joyful Gospel-teaching. Help me all the while to forgive those who sin against me, as I ask forgiveness for my sins as well.</p>
<p class="quote">I ask this for Your glory through my labor, Amen.</p>
